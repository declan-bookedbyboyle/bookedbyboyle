Got Questions?

FAQ

Is this really free for property owners?

Yes. 100%. Our fees are paid by the contractors who receive the booking — not by you. We believe property owners shouldn't have to pay to access a good contractor. You'll never see a bill from us.

How are contractors vetted for commercial work?

We verify license, insurance, years in business, and commercial property experience before any contractor can receive a commercial referral. We also track their performance over time — bad actors get removed from the network.

What areas do you cover?

We currently focus on Long Island (Nassau and Suffolk counties). If your property is just outside, submit a request anyway — our contractor network sometimes extends further and we'll tell you honestly whether we can help.

What if I manage multiple properties?

Great — that's exactly our sweet spot. Property managers with multiple sites get a single point of contact (Declan) who handles bid coordination across all properties and all trades. Submit multiple requests or reach out directly to set up a preferred-manager arrangement.

How fast will I actually hear back?

We guarantee first contact within 72 hours. Most requests get a response from Declan the same day. Urgent projects (active leaks, safety issues) get bumped to same-day treatment — just note it in your description.
