Homeowner FAQ

Is this really free for me?

Yes, completely. You pay nothing — before, during, or after. We also never affect what the contractor quotes you. The price they give you is the only price you pay.

Will my info be sold or shared?

Never sold. Sharing with contractors is your choice — you pick on the form. Either way, we act as your only point of contact. No contractor reaches out without your permission.

How fast will my appointments be set?

We target 72 hours from when you submit. We reach out to contractors right away and coordinate around your availability. You'll hear back from us — not from a contractor — with confirmed times.

How many quotes can I get?

You choose: 1, 2, or 3. We spread them across your available days. Each contractor gets their own dedicated time slot — no pile-ons. If your schedule changes, no problem.

How are contractors vetted?

We verify licensing and insurance for every contractor. We only book established local businesses — no out-of-town outfits, no storm chasers.

Do you raise my quote price?

No. Your quote comes directly from the contractor — we have zero involvement in their pricing. The number they give you is exactly what they'd give anyone.

What if a contractor doesn't show?

Let us know immediately and we'll fix it. Contractors know we hold them accountable — it's a big reason no-shows are rare when you go through us.

Am I obligated to hire anyone?

Not at all. Compare quotes at your own pace and choose whoever feels right — or no one. Zero obligation, zero pressure.
