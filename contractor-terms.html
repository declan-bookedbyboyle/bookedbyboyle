Parties

This Agreement is entered into between:

  • Booked by Boyle ("Company"), located at 37 Irwin Place, Huntington, NY 11743; and
  • The independent contractor signing below ("Contractor").
1

Services

Company operates a lead generation platform that connects residential homeowners and commercial property owners with qualified contractors. Company will provide Contractor with qualified job leads in Contractor's trade and service area. Each lead includes full job details, scope, location, property type, and urgency before Contractor is asked to commit to any payment.

2

Fees and Payment

Appointment Fee

Contractor agrees to pay the agreed-upon appointment fee for each lead accepted. The fee amount is set by trade and job type and is displayed on the job card before Contractor commits.

Backend Fee

If Contractor is awarded the job, Contractor agrees to pay a backend percentage of the total contract value as shown in the current pricing schedule on Company's platform. This includes any change orders or scope expansions agreed to after the original contract. For purposes of this Agreement, "substantial completion" means the earlier of: (a) the homeowner or property owner signs off on the completed work, or (b) final payment is collected from the customer — whichever occurs first.

  • Payment is due within 7 days of the earlier of: (a) final payment received from the customer, or (b) substantial completion of the contracted scope of work as defined above.
  • Fees unpaid after 7 days accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% per year) until paid in full.
  • Contractor is solely responsible for reporting the final job value honestly and completely. Intentional misrepresentation of job value constitutes a material breach of this Agreement and entitles Company to recover the unpaid fee plus a 25% penalty on the understated amount.
3

Non-Circumvention / No Poaching

Contractor agrees not to solicit, contact, or perform compensated work for any customer introduced by Company without paying all applicable fees. This obligation applies to:

  • Direct agreements, side arrangements, cash deals, or referrals to affiliates.
  • Any future work with the same customer at the same or any other property for a period of 24 months following the initial introduction by Company.

This Section 3 survives termination of this Agreement. Violation entitles Company to recover all unpaid fees plus reasonable attorney fees and costs of collection.

4

Contractor Responsibilities

  • Contractor must maintain all required licenses, permits, and insurance for the work performed at all times during this Agreement.
  • Contractor is fully responsible for all work quality, warranties, permits, code compliance, safety, and customer satisfaction.
  • Contractor must conduct themselves professionally with all customers and comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws.
  • Contractor must notify Company within 48 hours if any license or insurance lapses or is revoked.
5

Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, agency, or joint venture relationship between the parties. Contractor is solely responsible for their own taxes, workers' compensation, benefits, and insurance. Company has no right to control the manner or means by which Contractor performs the work.

6

Limitation of Liability

Company acts solely as a scheduling and referral intermediary. Company is not a party to any agreement between Contractor and a property owner. Company is not liable for any disputes, damages, injuries, property damage, delays, or claims of any kind arising from the actual performance or non-performance of work by Contractor.

In no event shall Company's total liability to Contractor under this Agreement exceed the total fees paid by Contractor to Company in the 90 days preceding the claim.

7

Dispute Resolution

Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration administered in Suffolk County, New York under the commercial arbitration rules of the American Arbitration Association. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees and costs. Nothing in this Section prevents either party from seeking emergency injunctive relief from a court of competent jurisdiction.

8

Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party with 14 days' written notice. Upon termination:

  • Contractor remains responsible for all outstanding fees on previously accepted leads regardless of when those jobs close.
  • Sections 3 (Non-Circumvention), 6 (Limitation of Liability), and 7 (Dispute Resolution) survive termination indefinitely.
  • Sections 2 (Fees), 4 (Contractor Responsibilities), 11 (Referral Program), and 12 (Cancellations and No-Shows) survive for all jobs accepted prior to the termination date.
  • Any unused joining credit under Section 10 is forfeited upon termination.
  • Any earned but unpaid referral payouts under Section 11 that predate termination will be honored provided no outstanding fees remain.
9

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Venue for any court proceedings shall be in Suffolk County, New York.

10

Joining Credit

$100 Founding Member Credit

Upon approval into the Booked by Boyle network, eligible Contractors will receive a one-time $100 credit applied automatically toward their first confirmed booking fee. This credit is issued at Company's discretion and subject to the following conditions:

  • Credit is issued once per Contractor regardless of the number of trades listed on the application.
  • Credit has no cash value and cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed for cash under any circumstances.
  • Credit applies only to the appointment fee on Contractor's first confirmed booking and cannot be split across multiple bookings.
  • Credit expires 90 days after the date of Contractor's approval if unused.
  • Credit is forfeited immediately if Contractor is removed from the network for any policy violation or if Contractor has outstanding unpaid fees at the time of first booking.
  • If the appointment fee on the first booking is less than $100, the remaining credit balance is forfeited — it does not carry over to future bookings.
  • Company reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the joining credit program at any time with 7 days' written notice to Contractors who have not yet used their credit.
11

Referral Program

$50 Cash Referral Payout

Upon approval, each Contractor is issued a unique referral code. When a homeowner or property owner submits a job request using Contractor's referral code and that job results in a completed booking with a collected appointment fee, Company will pay Contractor $50 cash via the payment method on file (Zelle, Venmo, or PayPal).

  • Referral code must be entered by the referred party at the time of form submission to be tracked and eligible for payout. Retroactive code additions are not honored.
  • Payout is triggered only when the appointment fee has been collected from the referred Contractor who accepted the job — not at the time of scheduling or form submission.
  • Each completed job counts as one referral payout. If a referred customer submits multiple jobs across different trades, each completed job generates a separate $50 payout.
  • Contractor may refer other contractors to the network. If a referred contractor completes their first booking, the referring Contractor earns a $50 cash payout under the same conditions above.
  • Referral payouts are issued within 14 days of the completed booking being confirmed by Company.
  • Referral payouts are forfeited if the referring Contractor has outstanding unpaid fees at the time of payout. Payouts will be held and applied against outstanding balances first.
  • Referral codes may not be used fraudulently, shared publicly in mass marketing, or applied to jobs where the referring Contractor is also the accepting Contractor on the same job.
  • Any suspected abuse, manipulation, or fraudulent use of referral codes will result in immediate forfeiture of all pending payouts and may result in termination of this Agreement.
  • Company reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue the referral program at any time with 7 days' written notice. Payouts already earned prior to the notice date will be honored.
12

Cancellations and No-Shows

Policy Overview

Both Contractors and property owners are held to the same standard of accountability. This section defines what happens when either party fails to honor a confirmed appointment.

  • Homeowner cancellation with less than 24 hours notice: Company will issue Contractor a full credit equal to the appointment fee paid, applied to their next confirmed booking.
  • Homeowner no-show with no notice: Company will issue Contractor a full credit equal to the appointment fee paid, applied to their next confirmed booking.
  • Contractor cancellation with less than 24 hours notice: Appointment fee is forfeited. No credit or refund will be issued. A pattern of late cancellations may result in removal from the network.
  • Contractor no-show with no notice: Appointment fee is forfeited in full. Company reserves the right to immediately suspend or terminate Contractor's access to the network.
  • Credits issued under this section have no cash value, do not expire, and apply automatically to the next confirmed booking.
  • Disputes regarding cancellation credits must be submitted to Company within 7 days of the missed appointment via email or text to the contact information listed in this Agreement.

Company may update the pricing schedule from time to time with 7 days' written notice. Contractor's continued acceptance of leads after such notice constitutes acceptance of the updated pricing.
